about us

JK2L2 is a company of IT experts and professionals that share one passion and goal to enable brands to thrive in the ever-evolving business landscape shaped by technological innovation. With over two decades of exceptional experience in Information Technology. Events Management, and Logistics, JK2L2 has proven instrumental in sharing its expertise to empower and lead businesses and enterprises across industries in their digital transformation journey.

As COVID-19 cases in the Philippines continue to rise, individuals, and institutions have had to take immediate actions and decisions in an uncharted territory to protect one another. In these unprecedented times, JK2L2 Inc. forged partnerships with various laboratories to offer individuals and institutions the "We Test As One" program. It springs from JK2L2's vision to provide accessible, affordable and accurate testing for every Filipino to help the nation heal as one. As responsible individuals, employers, business owners, and members of the community, getting tested is one of the many ways to help flatten the curve. By knowing and being aware of our state we gain the power to protect ourselves, our loved ones, and the people around us.

'We test as One' program provides the most comprehensive and convenient COVID-19 testing for residents and companies nationwide. It provides accurate results to ensure the safety and protection of everyone from the virus.
